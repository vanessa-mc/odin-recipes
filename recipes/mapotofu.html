Grandma's Mapo Tofu

Description

This is a simple version of my grandmother's mapo tofu recipe and it's my favorite! Recreate this recipe with or without the meat and then enjoy it with a fresh bowl of white rice. It's quick and easy, perfect for a fast weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • A pack of silken tofu
  • 1 pound of ground pork
  • 1 bunch of scallions
  • 1/2 a tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup or less of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat up the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook the ground pork until brown.
  3. Place the silken tofu in the middle of the pan and if you'd like you can break it up into smaller chunks.
  4. Pour the oyster sauce and soy sauce into the pan.
  5. Combine the cornstarch and water, then pour it into the pan.
  6. Wait for the food to simmer and cover the pan for a few minutes and then turn the stove off and let it sit covered for another two minutes.
