Healthy Banana Foster Toast

Description

This is my version of an easy and healthy bananas foster toast. I like to make this when I'm craving something sweet and healthy for breakfast, or any time of the day honestly. Everyone loves it!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Your favorite kind of bread
  • Peanut or nut butter
  • Chia seeds

Steps

  1. Slice the banana into 1/4 inch thick slices.
  2. Have a pan over medium heat.
  3. Put the coconut oil, vanilla extract, cinnamon powder, and maple syrup into the pan, stir it, and let it melt.
  4. Then place your banana slices into the mixture in the pan and let it simmer.
  5. Flip the banana slices and let it caramelize.
  6. Toast your bread and spread some peanut or nut butter on it.
  7. Place your caramelized banana slices onto the toast and sprinkle some chia seeds on top! Enjoy!
